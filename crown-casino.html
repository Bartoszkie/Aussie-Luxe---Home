Crown Casino Melbourne

The Crown Casino Melbourne is the largest casino in Australia, and acts as one of the major entertainment complexes in the city, offering a huge variety of shows, cinema, music and conferences. Set in fantastic surroundings along Yarra river in the heart of the city, The Crown casino is a stunning landmark.

The complex is so large that it actually comprises of 3 hotels, the pick being the 5 star Crown Towers which has 481 rooms with stunning views of the city. Many famous guests have stayed here in recent years, including Tom Cruise, Nicole Kidman, Kim Kardashian, and Tiger Woods. If you fancy rubbing shoulders with celebrities and perhaps grabbing an autograph, then this is definitely the place for you.

The Crown Casino offers a huge amount of entertainment on top of the vast gaming section. With several restaurants, nightclubs and designer shopping from the likes of Hugo Boss, Prada and Burberry, there is something for everyone.
