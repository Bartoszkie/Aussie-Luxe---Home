Jupiters Hotel & Casino

Located in the upmarket neighbourhood of Broadbeach on Queensland’s Gold Coast, The Jupiters Casino offers and Hotel offers a large variety of entertainment to suit every taste. The seven acre complex includes a luxurious hotel with almost 600 rooms, numerous restaurants and bars, theatre and health spa.

Having benefited from a multi-million dollar refurbishment in 2012, the Jupiters Casino offers an excellent gaming choice to rival even the most luxurious Vegas casinos. Open 24 hours a day, the gaming tables and machines offer games at stake levels to suit all guests.

The entertainment at Jupiters casino is widely regarded as being the best on the Gold Coast, with many high profile artists performing. The upcoming schedule of acts includes musical performances from the likes of the Pointer Sisters and Muse. Those who prefer a show can enjoy Draculas Cabaret or Cabaret De Paris. All of these acts perform in the state of the art theatre which can accommodate up to 2300, ad boasts cutting-edge sound technology.

There are many fine dining options available at Jupiters, with the most well-known being Kiyomi. The restaurant and bar serves a modern, yet distinctly Japanese menu created by internationally recognised Executive Head Chef Chase Kojima. Kiyomi celebrates the experience of shared dining with an exciting food and beverage menu including a wide variety of Japanese tap and bottled beers, sake, shochu cocktails and Japanese whiskies.
